About SuccorTrail

A revolutionary approach to food aid distribution powered by blockchain technology

SuccorTrail addresses critical challenges in food aid distribution by leveraging blockchain technology to create an immutable, transparent record of every transaction in the supply chain.

Our platform connects donors directly with recipients, reducing waste and ensuring that resources reach those who need them most. Every meal package is tracked from source to recipient with verifiable data.
